Welcome!

Welcome to The Company Game, here is how to play.

Basics

You start with $1000. This money can be spent on 10 different companies all ranging in price and payout. The more the company costs the more effiecient the cost will be compared to the payout. Rememer this that it is worth it to buy the more expensive companies. Than once you have bought different companies you can see how much money will be gained by going to the next day. This can be done by pressing on the next day button.

Prestiging

This is a system that will reset your progress, but with a twist. This twist is that you will gain prestige points. These points will give you a .001 multiplier. This multiplier applies to both your starting amount of money and on your income for the day.

Spending Restige Points

Once you have clelect prestige points you can spend them on multipliers for everything or specific companies. However, this will alter your base multipier from prestige points so keep that in mind.
